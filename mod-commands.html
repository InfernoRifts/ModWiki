General Mod Commands

Common moderation commands and short descriptions. Use the Copy button to copy a command.

  • /vanish

    Vanish from player sight. Useful for observing without being noticed.

  • /gmsp

    Put yourself in spectator mode.

  • /tp <player>

    Teleport directly to a player.

  • /tp here <player>

    Teleport a player to your location — useful for checking AFK clicking or quick inspections.

  • /invsee <player>

    View a player's inventory or give replacement items if needed.

  • /ban <player>

    Ban a player from the server. Confirm the username and reason before executing.

  • /mute <player> <time>

    Mute a player. Time format: m (minutes), h (hours), d (days), w (weeks), mo (months), y (years). Example: /mute Player123 30m.

  • /jail <player> <cell number> <time>

    Jail a player in a specific cell for a duration. Time format as in /mute. Example: /jail Griefer42 2 1h.

Notes & best practices

  • Double-check player names and durations before running punitive commands.
  • Record actions in mod logs and, when applicable, notify senior staff.
  • Time formats accept suffixes: m, h, d, w, mo, y (minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, years).
